FMCG & Retail

In the FMCG and retail industry, we support companies in turning operational and commercial data into strategic advantage. Our expertise covers pricing, promotions, sales performance, supply chain analytics, and margin optimization, helping clients achieve full visibility across their value chain. Through dashboards, automation, and data-driven insights, we enable management teams to track execution, evaluate performance, and improve profitability — ensuring stronger alignment between strategy, operations, and market execution.

Services for FMCG & Retail

  1. Sales Dashboard → Track sales YTD, MTD, daily vs budget, targets, and last year, including margin.
  2. Price Elasticity Dashboard → Forecast sales performance by modeling price sensitivity and promotion effectiveness.
  3. Volume Dashboard → Monitor product, category, and channel volumes with drill-downs.
  4. Target Phasing → Align monthly and weekly sales targets with budgets and long-term plans.
  5. Project Trackers → Dedicated dashboards for initiatives like Ramadan, Back-to-School, and seasonal campaigns.
  6. Availability Dashboard → Measure product availability by region, store, and channel.
  7. Inventory Management Dashboard → Track aged stock, turnover, and supply chain efficiency.
  8. Supply Chain ERP Dashboard → Integrate with ERP to show procurement, logistics, and stock flows.
  9. Monthly Sales Closing Dashboard → Automate month-end closing with variance vs targets and margin analysis.
  10. P&L Dashboard → Consolidated profitability across sales, rebates, discounts, and costs.
  11. Margin Analysis Dashboard → Supplier evaluation and margin optimization by product/channel.
  12. Space, Range & Display Dashboard → Monitor assortment, planograms, and execution by region/store.
  13. Promotions Dashboard → Full suite to track slots, campaigns, locations, and ROI of offers.
  14. Weekly Promotion Tracker → Track execution/performance of leaflet items vs plan.
  15. Ad Hoc Business Dashboards → Rapidly develop dashboards for emerging management requirements.
  16. Automation & Centralization → Automate workflows and centralize reporting to cut manual effort.
  17. Price Mix Effect Analysis → Assess whether growth is driven by premium vs cheaper products and quantify revenue/margin impact from new or discontinued items.
  18. Advertising & Promotion ROI via ML Regression → Use ML to identify the best promotion mix; compare predicted vs actual results in dashboards.
  19. Pricing Impact Dashboard → Measure the impact of pricing tool submissions on sales & margins; compare before/after performance and flag risk areas.
  20. Pricing Tools (Excel Automation) → Excel-based tools to automate pricing submissions, streamline approvals, and publish to ERP/Power BI.
  21. Automation on Demand → Build fit-for-purpose tools (Excel, Power BI, Python) for ad‑hoc automation requests aligned to business need.

Insurance

We partner with insurers to improve profitability, pricing discipline, and portfolio visibility. Our approach blends actuarial rigor with executive-ready dashboards — so leaders can steer pricing, govern discounts, and act on emerging risks with confidence.

Services for Insurance

  1. Pricing Tools for Underwriting → Custom tools (Excel, Power BI, Python) combining actuarial pricing, system pricing, and underwriting judgment into a single framework; plus simplified versions for Sales to generate indicative prices.
  2. Power BI / Excel Distribution Tools → Secure templates and dashboards for underwriting and sales teams with controlled access and consistent definitions.
  3. Loss Ratio & Portfolio Monitoring → Track all lines, new/renewal mixes, claims, and financial ratios; executive summaries with operational drill-downs.
  4. Performance Analysis & Root Cause → Identify drops vs last year, target, or plan; isolate pricing, claims, or mix drivers and recommend corrective actions.
  5. Integration of Underwriting Outputs → Feed pricing and risk-selection outputs into enterprise dashboards for governance and transparency.
  6. Actuarial Support & Data Preparation → Automate adequacy testing, regulatory submissions (burning cost, frequency, severity), and recurring reports.
  7. Reserving & Chain Ladder Automation → Automate chain-ladder tables and reserve monitoring to increase speed and consistency.
  8. Machine Learning for Risk Assessment → Predict claims, segment portfolios, and visualize insights for adoption by underwriting and management.
  9. Sales Enablement & Risk Appetite Alignment → Provide pre-underwriting pricing tools and align proposals to risk appetite and governance thresholds.
  10. Executive Oversight & Project Dashboards → Portfolio trend monitors and initiative trackers; include market competitiveness benchmarks when relevant.
